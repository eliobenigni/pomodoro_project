The Pomodoro App is a simple productivity tool designed to help users manage their time more effectively using the Pomodoro Technique. It features a countdown timer set to a default duration of 25 minutes, known as a "Pomodoro session," followed by a short break. The app displays the remaining time dynamically, updating every second, and plays a notification sound when the session is complete. Users can start the timer by clicking the "Start" button, initiating a focused work session. This app aims to enhance productivity by encouraging users to work in short, focused bursts, followed by regular breaks, to maintain concentration and motivation throughout their tasks.
